Biomimicry of The Venus's Flower Basket

Euplectella Aspergillum

This deep water sponge has tubelike skeleton forms a square grid with diagonal reinforcements, often compared to human-made lattice structures which are typically seen in bridge structures.

POTENTIAL USES:
Lighter yet more durable packaging with the incorporation of the silica used in sea sponges from its surrounding environments.

Venus's flower basket has a highly intricate and strong skeletal structure. You can draw inspiration from its geometric patterns to design packaging that is not only visually appealing but also structurally robust, ensuring the protection of the contents.
