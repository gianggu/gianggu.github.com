History of Internet

October 18, 2023

The internet's advancements have been building off of its foundations from its very beginning in 1969 with its creation of TCP/IPs and packet switching. The makings of faster internet and better connection across multiple networks have been improving very quickly since the year 2000, where newer devices other than computers can operate just as efficiently.
