Eggs, Bacon, and Broccoli

INGREDIENTS
  • 1 Egg
  • Rice
  • 3 Small Bell Peppers Or 1/2 Of A Bell Pepper
  • 2-3 Strips Of Bacon
  • 1/4 Yellow Onion
  • Black Pepper
  • Kosher Salt
  • Garlic Flakes
  • 1 Tablespoon Of Olive Oil
  1. Cook your rice before cooking your other ingredients. Here’s a step-by-step if you need some help.
  2. Cut your bell peppers and onion into blocks and leave it on the side.
  3. Place your pan at medium high heat. Once the pan is hot, add your bacon.
  4. After 4-5 minutes or once the bacon starts to buckle and curl, flip to the other side.
  5. While the bacon is cooking, add your bell peppers.
  6. Sprinkle some black pepper, kosher salt, and garlic flakes into the pan and mix the contents.
  7. After a few more minutes of light mixing, pour the food into a bowl.
  8. Add olive oil to your bacon grease in the pan.
  9. Crack your egg into the pan, let it crisp up at the edges, and sprinkle some salt on top.
  10. Once it’s ready, scoop in some rice and add it to your bowl!
