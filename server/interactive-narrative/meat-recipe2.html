Sichuan Pork Belly

INGREDIENTS
  • 4 Tbsp Of Oil
  • 1 Lb of Pork Belly
  • 4 Tbsp Korean Chili Flakes
  • Sesame Seeds
  • 1 Tsp Sichuan Peppers
  • A Couple of Sichuan Chilis
  • Ginger Honey/Sugar
STEPS
  1. Cut your vegetables into blocks and leave it on the side.
  2. Place your pan at medium high heat. Once the pan is hot, add your mixed vegetables.
  3. Add your meat and let it brown on one side. Add your seasonings into the pan.
  4. Pour a generous amount of honey on top and mix it with your meat and vegetables.
  5. After a few minutes of light mixing, pour the food into a bowl.
  6. Bring the pan into a medium low and add a little oil. Cook your egg with the honey and grease residue.
  7. Sprinkle some salt, scoop in some rice and add it to your bowl!
