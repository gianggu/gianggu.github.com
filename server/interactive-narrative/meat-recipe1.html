Shaved Steak and Tomatoes

INGREDIENTS
  • 12 Oz. Shaved Beef Steak
  • 1 Cup Of Grape Tomatoes
  • 1 Tbsp of Salt
  • Coriander Powder
  • Ginger Powder
  • Curry Powder
  • Rosemary Flakes
  • 1 Tbsp of Olive Oil
STEPS
  1. Heat your pan at medium heat and add your oil to the pan.
  2. Add your tomatoes in and cook for a few minutes at medium low heat until they start to deflate.
  3. Add your meat and let it brown on one side. Add your seasonings into the pan to your liking
  4. Scoop in some rice and add it to your bowl!
