Sunny Side Up and Miscellaneous

This is a recipe that works with most vegetables and meat you have lying in your fridge. I made this when I wanted to get rid of my wilting vegetables. If you’re trying to just throw something together but still have it delicious then follow this recipe!

INGREDIENTS
  • 1 Egg
  • Rice
  • 3 Small Bell Peppers Or 1/2 Of A Bell Pepper
  • 1/4 Yellow Onion
  • Cabbage
  • Any Cut Of Meat (I Chose Leftover Hot Pot Meat)
  • Black Pepper
  • Minced Garlic
  • 4 Tbsp Of Ginger Honey
  • 1 Tablespoon Of Olive Oil
STEPS
  1. Cook your rice before cooking your other ingredients. Here’s a step-by-step if you need some help.
  2. Cut your vegetables into blocks and leave it on the side.
  3. Place your pan at medium high heat. Once the pan is hot, add your mixed vegetables.
  4. Add your meat and let it brown on one side. Add your seasonings into the pan.
  5. Pour the honey on top and mix it with your meat and vegetables.
  6. After a few minutes of light mixing, pour the food into a bowl.
  7. Bring the pan into a medium low and add a little oil. Cook your egg with the honey and grease residue.
  8. Sprinkle some salt, scoop in some rice and add it to your bowl!
